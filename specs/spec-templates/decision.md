## About Decision Records

Decision records preserve the reasoning behind important choices. Their purpose is to capture the context, alternatives, decision, and consequences of a choice so that future contributors understand not only what was decided, but why it was decided. These documents help prevent teams from repeatedly revisiting the same discussions and provide historical context for architectural and product decisions. When asking "Why was this done this way?", the answer should be found in a decision record.
